# ListingIgnite – Brand Voice Guidelines

This file defines how ListingIgnite communicates — in UI copy, marketing emails,
landing pages, error messages, button labels, and any other written content.
Claude Code and Claude should reference this file when writing or editing any
copy for ListingIgnite.

---

## Brand Personality

ListingIgnite is a tool serious agents trust. The brand feels like a smart,
efficient colleague who respects your time — not a hype machine, not a corporate
robot, and not an overly casual buddy. Premium, fast, and results-focused.

---

## Six Core Brand Voice Principles

### 1. Confident, not cocky
State what the product does. Don't over-qualify. Don't apologize.
- ✅ "Get your full listing kit in seconds."
- ❌ "We try to help you generate your listing materials quickly."

### 2. Agent-first language
Speak the way a top-producing agent thinks. Agents don't want to think about
AI — they want results.
- ✅ "Close more listings. Market faster."
- ❌ "Generate AI content for your real estate business."

### 3. Speed is the value
Time is always part of the message. Every major CTA should gesture at speed.
It's the core product promise.
- ✅ "In seconds." "Instantly." "Done."
- ❌ Anything that implies effort, waiting, or complexity

### 4. Short sentences. Active verbs.
Real estate agents are busy. Copy should be skimmable. Lead with the verb.
- ✅ "Paste your details. Get everything."
- ❌ "By simply entering your property information, the system will automatically generate..."

### 5. Premium, not hype
Avoid exclamation points, ALL CAPS, and hyperbolic claims. The premium feel
comes from restraint.
- ✅ "Your listing, fully marketed."
- ❌ "GENERATE INSANE AI LISTING CONTENT!!!"

### 6. Warm but not casual
Don't use slang or be overly familiar. But also don't be corporate-robotic.
The tone is a smart colleague who respects your time.
- ✅ "Here's your full marketing kit."
- ❌ "Hey! Your stuff is ready 🎉"

## On-Brand vs Off-Brand Examples

| On-Brand ✅ | Off-Brand ❌ |
|---|---|
| "Your listing. Fully marketed. In seconds." | "Leverage AI-powered tools to accelerate your real estate content workflows." |
| "Paste your details. We handle everything else." | "Start your free trial and discover amazing features today!" |
| "MLS copy, social posts, email blast — done." | "Our cutting-edge AI generates multiple types of marketing content for you!!" |
| "Serious agents use ListingIgnite." | "Hey there! Ready to supercharge your listings? Let's go! 🔥🔥🔥" |

## UI Copy Guidelines

- **Button labels:** Use active verbs. "Generate Marketing Content" not "Submit."
  "Get Started Free" not "Sign Up Now!"
- **Error messages:** Be direct and helpful. "Generation failed — please try again."
  Not "Oops! Something went wrong 😅"
- **Success messages:** Calm and confirmatory. "Your marketing content is ready."
  Not "Amazing! Your content has been generated! 🎉"
- **Empty states:** Encouraging but not cheesy. "No listings yet — create your first one."
  Not "Wow, it's empty here! Time to get started! 🚀"
- **Loading states:** Informative. "Generating your marketing content..."
  Not "Hang tight, magic is happening! ✨"
- **Placeholders:** Descriptive and practical. "e.g. Pool, Garage, Waterfront"
  Not "Tell us what makes this property special!"

## Words and Phrases to Use
- In seconds / Instantly / Done
- Your listing / Your marketing
- Paste and go / Ready to use
- Every format / All at once
- Serious agents
- Full marketing kit

## Words and Phrases to Avoid
- Revolutionary / Game-changing / Cutting-edge
- Supercharge / Turbocharge / Skyrocket
- Amazing / Incredible / Awesome (as filler)
- Leverage / Utilize / Synergize
- AI-powered (lead with the result, not the technology)
- Exclamation points (use sparingly, if ever)

---

## A Note on AI Mentions
ListingIgnite is powered by AI, but agents don't need to think about that.
Lead with outcomes, not technology. "Get your MLS description in seconds"
is stronger than "Our AI generates your MLS description."
Only mention AI when it builds trust or explains something — never as a
buzzword or selling point on its own.
