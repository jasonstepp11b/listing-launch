## Product Context

ListingIgnite is a SaaS tool for real estate agents. Paste in a property
listing and instantly get a full marketing kit:

- MLS description
- Social media posts (Facebook, Instagram, X)
- Email blast
- Flyer copy
- Video script (with YouTube metadata)
- SEO landing page copy

Agents currently spend 1-3 hours per listing creating this content manually.
ListingIgnite eliminates that entirely.

## X/Twitter Voice & Posting Rules

These rules are specific to X and supplement BRAND-VOICE.md:

### Never use em dashes
Do not use "—" in any post. Even though they are grammatically correct,
most people associate em dashes with AI-generated content. Use a regular
hyphen (-) or restructure the sentence instead.

### Keep posts short and punchy
X rewards brevity. Lead with the most interesting line. If someone stops
reading after the first sentence, they should still get the point.

### No hashtag stuffing
One or two relevant hashtags maximum per post, if any. Never end a post
with a wall of hashtags.

### No exclamation points
Per BRAND-VOICE.md. Confidence comes from restraint, not enthusiasm.

### No AI buzzwords
Never use: revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, supercharge,
leverage, utilize, AI-powered. Lead with outcomes, not technology.

### Active voice, short sentences
"Agents spend 2 hours per listing on marketing content." Not "Marketing
content creation is a time-consuming process for real estate agents."

### Threads are fine for longer content
If a topic needs more than 280 characters, use a thread. First post in
the thread should be strong enough to stand alone and make people want
to read more.
