## Product Context

ListingIgnite is a SaaS tool for real estate agents. Paste in a property
listing and instantly get a full marketing kit:

- MLS description
- Social media posts (Facebook, Instagram, X)
- Email blast
- Flyer copy
- Video script (with YouTube metadata)
- SEO landing page copy

Agents currently spend 1-3 hours per listing creating this content manually.
ListingIgnite eliminates that entirely.

Website: listingignite.com
Tagline: "List it. Ignite it."

## How Instagram Works for This Product

Instagram is a visual-first platform. Every post needs an image or graphic.
Text alone does not work here. The content strategy must lead with visuals
and use captions to add context and depth.

Real estate agents are highly active on Instagram. They use it to market
their own listings and personal brand. This makes them receptive to content
about listing marketing tools - they understand the pain point viscerally.

### Format priority order:
1. Carousels (swipeable multi-image posts) - highest engagement on Instagram
2. Single image posts - product screenshots, quote graphics, branded visuals
3. Reels - video content, lowest friction for demo content (future phase)

## Caption Voice & Rules

These supplement BRAND-VOICE.md for Instagram specifically:

### No em dashes
Do not use "—" in any caption. Restructure the sentence instead.
Most people associate em dashes with AI-generated content.

### No exclamation points
Per BRAND-VOICE.md. Confidence comes from restraint.

### No AI buzzwords
Never use: revolutionary, game-changing, cutting-edge, supercharge,
leverage, utilize, AI-powered. Lead with outcomes, not technology.

### Lead with the hook
Instagram captions get cut off after the first line before "more."
The first sentence must be strong enough to make someone tap "more."
Write the first line as if it is the only line they will read.

### Short paragraphs
Break captions into short paragraphs with line breaks. Dense walls
of text do not get read on Instagram. Two to three sentences per
paragraph maximum.

### Hashtags
Use 3-5 relevant hashtags per post, placed at the end of the caption.
Do not stuff hashtags. Quality over quantity.

Good hashtags for this account:
#realestate #realestateagent #realestatemarketing #listingagent
#realtorlife #realestatetools #proptech #mlslisting
#realestatelife #realtortips

## Engagement Strategy

### Follow before posting
Follow 30-50 relevant accounts before publishing anything:
- Active real estate agents who post regularly
- Real estate media accounts (Inman, The Close, NAR)
- Real estate marketing accounts
- PropTech accounts

### Comment strategy
Comments carry more weight than likes on Instagram.
Genuine comments on agent posts are the highest-leverage engagement activity.

Same rules as X engagement:
- Add something real or skip it
- Never "great post!" or empty affirmations
- Never pitch ListingIgnite in someone else's comments unprompted
- Answer questions agents ask in their own captions

### Daily rhythm
- Morning: like and comment on overnight posts from target accounts
- Midday: engage with fresh content
- Evening: respond to any comments on your own posts

30-45 minutes per day total. Consistency matters more than volume.

---

## Posting Frequency

Pre-launch target: 3-4 posts per week minimum.
Consistency matters more than frequency. A reliable 3x per week
beats a burst of 10 posts followed by silence.

## What to Create in Canva First

Priority order before starting to post:

1. Quote graphic template - branded background, large text, logo small
   in corner. Reusable for multiple posts.
2. Product screenshot frame - a simple branded border/frame to drop
   screenshots into. Makes raw app screenshots look polished.
3. Carousel template - consistent slide design for multi-image posts.
   Title slide, content slides, CTA slide.

## Reels (Future Phase)

Not a priority for launch. When ready:
- Screen recording of the full generation flow (paste property, watch
  everything generate) is the most compelling demo
- No face-on-camera required
- OBS Studio (free) or Loom for screen recording
- Light background music under voiceover
- Keep under 60 seconds for best reach

---

## Red Flags to Avoid

- Posting and disappearing - engagement drops fast without consistency
- Only posting promotional content - mix value-first content with CTAs
- Ignoring comments on your own posts - always reply
- Following and immediately DMing about ListingIgnite - build presence first
- Using the same caption format every post - vary the approach
